About Me

I'm currently 24 and was previously studying Psychology at the University of Washington, but due to some complications, I ended up not pursuing that field. I then started working at Amazon and have been working here for around 2 years now, and because of that, I was provided the opportunity to enroll in the Correlation One program. Things I enjoy doing outside of work include watching anime/reading manga, going for walks, playing occasional video games, and trying out other new hobbies. My latest hobby that I have indulged in is custom keyboards, which is a very expensive hobby, but seeing as I don't spend too much money in other places, I feel a little less worried about buying components. I only have 2 keyboards that I have built and personally like, but I have several more coming over the course of this year and the next. I'll be sure to post photos of them as soon as I am done building them :D.
